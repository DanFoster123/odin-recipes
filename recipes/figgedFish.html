Figged Fish


Description

Flakey white fish with warmed figs, basted in brandy and honey.

Ingredients

  • 8 fillets of white fish
  • 1 Tbsp flour
  • 4 Tbsp butter
  • 4 large ripe figs
  • 4 cups fresh sugar snap peas
  • 1 sliced lemon
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 2 Tbsp honey
  • 2 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 Tbsp brandy

Directions

  1. Heat 4 Tbsp butter in large saute pan on medium heat.
  2. Sprinkle fish with flour and pan fry in the butter until golden. Place on warm serving dish.
  3. Melt 2 Tbsp butter and honey in saute pan used to cook fish.
  4. Add lemon juice and whisk to blend.
  5. Cut figs in half and set cut side down in pan. Shake pan to keep figs from sticking.
  6. Add sliced lemon and peas. Sautee 4-5 minute until bubbly.
  7. Remove figs, lemons, and peas from pan and arrange on serving pan with fish.
  8. Add brandy to pan and whisk to incorporate. Once smooth, pour over fish and figs.
