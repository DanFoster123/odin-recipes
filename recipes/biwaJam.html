Biwa Jam


Description

Biwa, loquat, japanese plum. This golden fruit goes by many names, but is always delicious. Peel it, seed it, and jam it and you have a wonderful vibrant spread to bring a pinch of summer into the winter months.

Ingredients

  • Fresh loquats about 2 pounds
  • Granulated sugar, about a cup
  • juice of half a lemon

  • Pot
  • Sterilized jars
  • means to puree

Directions

  1. Rinse fruit.
  2. Half loquats and remove seed and inner skin around seed.
  3. Cut in half into half to finish wuartering fruit.
  4. Add sugr, lemon juice, and fruit to pot. Stir.
  5. Heat sterilize jars and set aside.
  6. Cook fruit until fruit is soften. Higher temps will be faster, but darken and thicken the jam, lower temps will be more yellow and softer jam.
  7. Puree and cook until consistency is to your liking.
  8. Ladle into warm jars leaving half inch of headspace. Seal and let cool completely before refrigerating.
